About CoFocus

CoFocus is a productivity and co-working application designed to help users stay focused while studying or working online.


Brand Identity

The visual identity of CoFocus is centered around minimalism, focus, and calm digital collaboration. The platform uses purple as its primary color because purple is commonly associated with wisdom, curiosity, independence, and stability.

As a balance between the energy of red and the calmness of blue, purple reflects both productivity and concentration. The color also represents creativity and inspiration, supporting the idea of a peaceful but motivating study environment.

The circular logo represents focus, direction, and shared goals. Its centered structure symbolizes concentration and balance, while the overall minimalist design reflects clarity and simplicity.

The slogan “Focus better. Together.” communicates the core purpose of CoFocus: creating a supportive digital environment where users can stay motivated and productive together.

Trademark Considerations

The name “CoFocus” and the visual identity of the project were researched using trademark databases including EUIPO eSearch and TMview. During the research process, no identical active trademark with the same branding purpose and market focus was identified.

The project branding was designed independently. The logo, colour palette, and geometric visual style were created to establish a unique and recognizable identity focused on productivity, collaboration, and calm digital environments.

The project does not intentionally imitate or reproduce the branding, trademarks, logos, or visual identity of existing companies or software platforms.
