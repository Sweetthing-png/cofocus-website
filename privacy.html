Privacy Policy for CoFocus

Last Updated: 16.05.2026

This Privacy Policy explains how CoFocus processes personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR / DSGVO) and the Austrian Data Protection Act (DSG).


1. Controller (Verantwortlicher)

The person responsible for data processing on this platform is:

CoFocus App Team
Techhubstraße 1
1010 Vienna, Austria
Email: privacy@cofocus-study.app


2. Data Collection and Purpose

CoFocus processes personal data only to the extent necessary to provide and improve the platform and its services.

A. Performance of Contract (Art. 6 (1) (b) GDPR)

To provide study and productivity services, manage user accounts, and support collaborative features, the following data may be processed:

  • Name / Username: To identify users within study sessions and collaborative features.
  • Email Address: For authentication, account management, and important service-related communication.
  • Usage Data: To save productivity sessions, focus milestones, and platform preferences.

B. Legitimate Interests (Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR)

For the technical operation, stability, and security of the platform, the following information may be processed:

  • IP Address: To detect misuse, fraud, or cyberattacks.
  • Technical Statistics: Browser type, operating system, and device-related information used to optimize website functionality and display.

C. Consent (Art. 6 (1) (a) GDPR)

Based on the user's active choice through the cookie consent banner, CoFocus may process:

  • Preference Cookies: To remember user interface and productivity settings.
  • Analytics Data: To understand how visitors interact with the platform and improve usability and performance.

3. Your Rights Under Data Protection Law

Under the GDPR, users have the following rights regarding their personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request information about the personal data processed by CoFocus.
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Request deletion of personal data and account information.
  • Right to Restriction: Request limited processing of personal data under certain circumstances.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive personal data in a structured and machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: Object to data processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw cookie consent at any time.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: Users may file a complaint with the Austrian Data Protection Authority (Datenschutzbehörde).

4. Cookies and Local Storage

CoFocus uses cookies and local browser storage to improve functionality, usability, and security.

  • Necessary Cookies: Required for login sessions, authentication, and essential platform security. These cookies do not require consent.
  • Optional Cookies: Used for analytics and remembering interface preferences. These cookies are only activated after user consent through the cookie banner.

Users may accept or decline optional cookies through the cookie consent banner displayed on the website.


5. Third-Party Services and Data Transfer

In collaborative features such as “Study with Friends”, usernames and online status may be visible to other participants within the same study session.

Personal data is not sold to third parties.


6. Data Security

CoFocus applies appropriate technical and organizational measures (TOMs) to protect personal data from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure.

These measures may include:

  • Secure authentication practices
  • Restricted access to personal information
  • SSL/TLS encrypted communication where applicable
  • Secure password storage and hashing methods

7. Data Retention

Personal data is stored only for as long as necessary to provide services, fulfill legal obligations, or respond to legitimate user requests.

If a user deletes their account, personal data may be removed within 30 days unless legal obligations require longer storage periods.


8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect legal, technical, or organizational changes to the platform and its services.

Significant changes affecting the processing of personal data may be communicated through email notifications or notices within the platform.
